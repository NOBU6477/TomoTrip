Frequently Asked Questions

Is TomoTrip a tour company?
No. TomoTrip connects travelers with local individuals across Japan. We are a matching platform, not a tour operator.
Can I contact in English?
Yes. English inquiries are fully supported. Our team will respond in English.
Are guides available only in major cities?
No. Guides are available nationwide, including local and rural areas. Whether you want to explore Tokyo or discover hidden villages in the countryside, we can help you find the right local friend.
